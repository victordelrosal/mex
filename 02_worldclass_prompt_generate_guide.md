# Prompt: Generate a Comprehensive Guide for Irish Fans Attending the World Cup in Mexico City

You are an expert travel writer, football tournament logistics analyst, and Mexico travel specialist.

Your task is to create the **definitive practical guide for Irish football supporters traveling to Mexico City for a FIFA World Cup match involving Ireland.**

The guide should be written clearly and practically for readers from Ireland who may be visiting Mexico for the first time.

---

# Objectives

Create a guide that:

- Helps Irish fans plan their trip with confidence
- Explains match logistics clearly
- Provides trustworthy travel advice
- Highlights authentic Mexican experiences
- Prioritizes safety and practical information
- Uses the most up-to-date information available

---

# Audience

Irish football supporters traveling from Ireland or the UK.

Assume the reader:

- Has never been to Mexico
- Speaks English only (or minimal Spanish)
- Wants practical, trustworthy advice
- Is traveling primarily for the football match

---

# Output Format

Produce the guide using **clear sections and sub-sections**.

Use a structure similar to a professional travel handbook.

Use bullet points where appropriate.

Avoid fluff. Prioritize useful information.

---

# Required Sections

## 1. Overview
Brief introduction explaining why Mexico City is an incredible place to experience a World Cup match.

## 2. Key Match Information
Explain:
- Stadium location
- Capacity
- Matchday logistics
- Arrival times
- Security checks
- Ticket procedures
- Transportation considerations

## 3. Getting to Mexico City
Include:
- Flight routes from Ireland and the UK
- Typical flight duration
- Price expectations
- Arrival airport logistics
- Immigration process

## 4. Best Areas to Stay
Explain neighborhoods such as:
- Roma
- Condesa
- Polanco
- Centro Histórico

Discuss:
- Safety
- Atmosphere
- Distance to stadium
- Price expectations

## 5. Transportation in Mexico City
Explain:
- Uber
- Metro
- Taxi safety
- Traffic considerations
- Matchday transportation strategy

## 6. Food Every Visitor Must Try
Introduce iconic Mexican foods such as:
- Tacos al pastor
- Tacos de suadero
- Tortas
- Tamales
- Chilaquiles

Include tips on:
- Safe street food
- Food markets
- Popular neighborhoods for food

## 7. Football Atmosphere
Explain:
- Where fans gather
- Sports bars
- Public viewing culture
- Fan zones if applicable

## 8. Safety Tips
Provide clear, practical advice including:
- Pickpocket awareness
- Neighborhood awareness
- Night travel
- Transportation safety

## 9. Cultural Tips
Explain:
- Mexican friendliness
- Social etiquette
- Tipping
- Basic cultural expectations

## 10. Useful Spanish Phrases
Include a short list useful for travelers.

## 11. Things to Do in Mexico City
Recommend highlights such as:
- Zócalo
- Chapultepec Park
- Teotihuacán pyramids
- Museums
- Markets

---

# Tone

Write in a tone that is:
- Friendly
- Knowledgeable
- Practical
- Culturally respectful

Think of the guide as **advice from a knowledgeable local friend**.

---

# Final Output

Produce a **complete, structured travel guide** suitable for publication or distribution to Irish supporters traveling to Mexico City.
