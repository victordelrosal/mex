# Deep Research Prompt
## Irish Fans Travel Guide – Mexico City World Cup Match

Conduct a **deep research analysis** to gather the most current and reliable information available for Irish supporters traveling to Mexico City for a FIFA World Cup match involving Ireland.

The purpose of this research is to support the creation of a **highly accurate and practical travel guide**.

---

# Research Requirements

Prioritize information from:

- FIFA official publications
- Mexican government tourism authorities
- Mexico City government
- Airport authorities
- Official stadium sources
- Trusted travel advisories
- Reliable transportation providers

Focus on the **most recent information available.**

---

# Research Topics

## 1. Stadium and Match Logistics
Identify:
- Official stadium name
- Capacity
- Matchday entry procedures
- Security requirements
- Ticket policies
- Fan zones
- Expected arrival times

## 2. Airport & Arrival Logistics
Research:
- Mexico City International Airport procedures
- Immigration process
- Arrival wait times
- Transportation options from airport

## 3. Transportation in Mexico City
Identify:
- Safe transportation methods
- Metro system usage
- Uber availability
- Matchday traffic patterns
- Travel times to stadium

## 4. Recommended Neighborhoods for Visitors
Analyze neighborhoods suitable for tourists including:
- Roma
- Condesa
- Polanco
- Centro Histórico

Evaluate:
- Safety
- Hotel availability
- Price ranges
- Distance to stadium

## 5. Accommodation Availability
Research:
- Hotel availability patterns during major events
- Price ranges during international tournaments
- Booking strategies

## 6. Safety and Travel Advisories
Collect guidance from:
- Mexican authorities
- Irish Department of Foreign Affairs
- Travel safety experts

Include:
- Areas to avoid
- Common tourist risks
- Practical safety recommendations

## 7. Food and Cultural Experiences
Identify:
- Authentic Mexican dishes
- Popular food neighborhoods
- Food markets
- Recommended restaurants

## 8. Fan Experience
Research:
- Football fan culture in Mexico City
- Typical matchday atmosphere
- Bars and fan gathering areas
- Public viewing events

---

# Output Format

Produce a **structured research briefing** including:

- Key findings
- Important statistics
- Official guidance
- Practical recommendations

The briefing should serve as **source material for creating a high-quality travel guide.**
