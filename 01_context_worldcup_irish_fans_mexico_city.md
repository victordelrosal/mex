# Context Brief
## Irish Fans Guide to the World Cup Match in Mexico City

### Purpose of This Document
This document provides the context and origin of a project to create a **practical guide for Irish football fans traveling to Mexico City** for a FIFA World Cup match involving Ireland and Mexico.

The intention is to develop a **high-quality travel and logistics guide** that helps Irish supporters prepare for and enjoy their trip to Mexico City, with reliable, up-to-date information and insider cultural knowledge.

This guide will later serve as the **template for similar guides for Guadalajara and Monterrey**, the other Mexican host cities.

---

# Origin of the Idea

The project originated from a real-world conversation with a long-time barber in Castlebar, County Mayo, Ireland.

The barber mentioned that if Ireland qualifies for the upcoming FIFA World Cup match against Mexico in Mexico City, he intends to travel there to watch the match in person.

During the conversation, several topics emerged:

- Flights to Mexico City (for example Heathrow → Mexico City direct flights around €800)
- Where to stay in the city
- Food recommendations
- Transportation logistics
- Safety considerations
- Cultural tips
- FIFA match logistics

This sparked the idea of building a **comprehensive guide for Irish supporters**, since many Irish fans may be traveling to Mexico for the first time.

---

# Project Objective

Create a **clear, practical, and culturally informed guide for Irish fans traveling to Mexico City for the World Cup match**.

The guide should:

- Help fans plan their trip easily
- Reduce uncertainty and stress
- Provide trustworthy advice
- Highlight authentic Mexican culture
- Help visitors enjoy the experience safely

---

# Target Audience

Primary audience:

Irish football supporters traveling to Mexico City for the match.

Typical characteristics:

- Likely first-time visitors to Mexico
- Traveling from Ireland or the UK
- Interested in football culture, food, and social atmosphere
- May have limited Spanish language ability
- Want practical, trustworthy guidance
